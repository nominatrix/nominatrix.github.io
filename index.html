A Little About Me

In the first twelve years of my professional life, I was an executive assistant with roles in the finance, real estate, and commercial food industries. In the four years before launching my freelancing career, I worked for the sales and marketing department of a large biotech firm. Take it from me: if you ever want to learn a lot about an industry, sitting behind an admin desk is a great place to start.

I went home at night to work on passion projects: blogs, personal essay, and novel-length literary fiction. I used narrative to bring worlds to life, and conversational writing to make blog readers laugh— and think.

A few years ago, I had the opportunity to combine these two experiences into my ideal role: writing exciting and engaging content for clients in a variety of industries. It’s the perfect intersection of my skills, and I love using them to help clients get their stories heard.
