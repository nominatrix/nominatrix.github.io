About Me

In the first twelve years of my professional life, I was an executive assistant with roles in finance, real estate, dairy, and biotech firms. Take it from me: if you ever want to learn a lot about an industry, a great place to start is behind an admin desk. At night I went home and worked on passion projects: blogs, stories, and novel-length literary fiction. I used narrative to bring stories to life, and conversational writing to make blog readers laugh, and think. A few years ago, I had the opportunity to combine these two experiences into my ideal role: writing exciting and engaging content for clients in a variety of industries. It’s the perfect intersection of my skills, and I love using them to help clients get their stories heard.
