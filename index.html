A little about me

After several years providing proposal and technical writing for a major biometric software firm, I now pursue projects closer to my heart. I offer ghostwriting services for new works, as well as developmental editing services to take your existing manuscript from final draft to first run. I hold a Bachelor of Science in English from Northeastern University.

When not refining narrative and cleaning up grammar, I am refining table manners and cleaning up toys for our eighteen-month-old triplets. In the golden hours after pajamas and bedtime stories, I love to write, knit, bake bread, and read classic and contemporary fiction.
